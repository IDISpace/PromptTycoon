Account & Data Deletion — Prompt Tycoon

Last updated: May 25, 2026

How to Delete Your Account and Data

Prompt Tycoon offers two levels of data deletion: deleting your cloud account and resetting all local game data. You can do either or both directly from within the app.

Option 1 — Delete Your Cloud Account (If You Signed In with Google)

  1. Open the Prompt Tycoon app on your device
  2. Tap the Settings icon (gear icon) on the Studio screen
  3. Scroll to the Account section
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm by typing DELETE when prompted

This action will:

  • Permanently delete your cloud save from our servers (Firebase Firestore)
  • Remove your account from Firebase Authentication
  • Sign you out of the app

Option 2 — Reset Local Game Progress

  1. Open the Prompt Tycoon app
  2. Tap the Settings icon (gear icon)
  3. Scroll to the Danger Zone section
  4. Tap Reset Game
  5. Confirm by typing RESET when prompted

This action will permanently delete all local game data including currency, owned items, upgrades, achievements, milestones, and prestige history stored on your device.

Option 3 — Delete Everything (Cloud + Local)

If you want to delete both your cloud save and local progress at once, use Settings → Danger Zone → Reset Everything (including cloud save). Confirm by typing WIPE.

What Data Is Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is removed:

  • Account identifier (Firebase UID)
  • Email address and display name (if signed in with Google)
  • All game progress (currency, items, upgrades, prestige)
  • Achievement and milestone progress
  • Daily login streak history
  • Game settings and preferences

What Data Is Retained

For up to 90 days after account deletion, we may retain anonymized server logs (without personal identifiers) for security and abuse prevention purposes. After 90 days, all data is permanently removed.

Some data may persist in third-party services we use:

  • Google AdMob: Device identifiers used for ad personalization are managed by Google. To delete, visit your Google Ads Settings: https://myadcenter.google.com
