Privacy Policy for Prompt Tycoon

Last updated: May 25, 2026

Introduction

Prompt Tycoon ("the App") is an idle clicker game developed by Mertkan B. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the App.

Information We Collect

Account Information (Optional)

If you choose to sign in with Google, we collect:

  • Your email address
  • Your display name
  • Your profile photo URL

This information is used solely to sync your game progress across devices.

Game Data

We store your game progress locally on your device and, if signed in, in Firebase Cloud Firestore. This includes:

  • In-game currency, owned items, upgrade levels
  • Achievement and milestone progress
  • Game settings and preferences

Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display rewarded ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data as described in Google's Privacy Policy.

Analytics

We do not collect any personal analytics data beyond what Firebase and AdMob services automatically provide.

How We Use Information

  • To sync your game progress across devices when you sign in
  • To display rewarded advertisements (if you choose to watch them)
  • To improve the app and fix bugs

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties beyond the services listed above (Google Firebase, Google AdMob).

Data Storage and Security

  • Game data is stored locally on your device using Hive database
  • Cloud data is stored in Google Firebase Cloud Firestore, protected by Firebase Authentication
  • Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS

Your Rights

You can:

  • Reset your local game progress at any time via Settings → Reset Game
  • Delete your cloud save and account via Settings → Delete Account
  • Continue playing without signing in (no data sent to cloud)

Children's Privacy

The App is rated for ages 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
